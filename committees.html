Committees

Committees turn membership into real work.

Committees are where members turn interest into action — shaping advocacy, education, membership outreach, and communications for the borough.

Standing committees

Advocacy Committee

Policy review, public statements, and legislative relationship work.

Education Committee

Programming design, event content, faculty outreach, and CME planning.

Membership Committee

Recruitment, onboarding, renewals, and member-experience improvements.

Residents & Trainees Committee

Trainee engagement, mentorship, and career-development programming.

Communications Committee

Newsletter, statements, social media, and site content governance.

Public Psychiatry Committee

Borough-facing work on community psychiatry, crisis services, and access.

Resident Scholarly Presentations Committee

Organizing the annual poster and oral competition across Brookdale, Maimonides, SUNY Downstate, and Interfaith residency programs since 1992.
