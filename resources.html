Patients & Families

Resources, referral guidance, and urgent support information.

This page helps patients, families, and community partners find psychiatric information, referral support, and crisis resources while staying connected to the society office when needed.

If you need urgent help

Call or text 988

The 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline provides free, confidential support 24/7.

For immediate danger or medical emergency

Call 911 or go to the nearest emergency department.

New York City support

NYC 988 can connect New Yorkers to crisis counseling, mobile crisis support, and behavioral health resources.
