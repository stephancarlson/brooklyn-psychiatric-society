Leadership & Board

Governance, continuity, and long service to Brooklyn psychiatry.

The Brooklyn Psychiatric Society depends on physician leadership, committee governance, trainee engagement, and experienced operational stewardship.

Executive leadership

Executive Director: Linda M. Majowka

Linda serves as the society's Executive Director and central operational contact, supporting membership, programming, communications, and continuity across decades of service.

Physician leadership

Society officers and executive council members guide annual priorities, represent the society publicly, and shape educational and advocacy work.

Governance structure

President

Leads annual priorities, public representation, and board direction.

President-Elect

Supports succession, programming continuity, and strategic planning.

Secretary / Treasurer

Supports governance records, reporting, and organizational stewardship.

Executive Council

Provides board-level oversight across membership, education, and advocacy.

Committee Chairs

Lead standing committees and translate mission into year-round work.

Trainee Leadership

Brings resident and fellow voices into programming, mentorship, and scholarship.
