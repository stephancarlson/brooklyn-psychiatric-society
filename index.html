Who We Are

A borough-level professional home for psychiatry.

Founded in 1949, the Brooklyn Psychiatric Society is the Kings County District Branch of the American Psychiatric Association and an affiliate of the New York State Psychiatric Association (NYSPA).

We represent psychiatrists training and practicing across Brooklyn’s four residency programs — Brookdale University Hospital, Maimonides Medical Center, SUNY Downstate Health Sciences University, and Interfaith Medical Center — and across the borough’s clinical and community settings.
