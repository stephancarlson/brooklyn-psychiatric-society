Membership

Join Brooklyn's professional home for psychiatry since 1949.

Membership in the Brooklyn Psychiatric Society connects you with borough-wide programming, advocacy, committee leadership, and professional community for practicing psychiatrists, trainees, and affiliate partners.

Psychiatrists Residents, Fellows, Students Affiliates

Membership paths

Full Member

For practicing psychiatrists seeking programming, committees, advocacy, and borough-specific networking.

Trainee Member

For residents, fellows, and medical students who want mentorship, presentation opportunities, and career support.

Affiliate Member

For aligned professionals and community partners who contribute to psychiatric education and access work.

What membership unlocks

Programs & Events

Priority access to annual meetings, borough briefings, mentorship nights, and committee programming.

Advocacy

A local platform to shape public statements on access, workforce, crisis care, and parity.

Professional visibility

Member spotlights, directory placement, and opportunities to present or moderate society sessions.

Committees

Structured entry points for advocacy, membership, education, communications, and trainee leadership work.

Join or renew flow

Step 1

Confirm member type and dues path.

Step 2

Create or activate your member profile for event registration, committees, and member communications.

Step 3

Complete dues/payment and route new members into communications and committee interest forms.
